PROJA

description

delicious corn-bread-like dish, perfect for a light breakfast or dinner

ingredients

  • 12 soup spoons of proja (corn) flour
  • 4 tablespoons of white (wheat) flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup (2dl) of yogurt
  • 1 cup (2dl) of mineral water
  • 250 grams of white cheese (optional)
  • 1 dl of sunflower oil
  • 1 bag of baking powder

steps

  1. mix all the ingredients written above in a large bowl, but do not chop the cheese too much - it is better when larger pieces remain
  2. grease the tray with melted margarine (or molds for bread and muffins, which I often use) and roll out the dough
  3. bake for 30 minutes in a preheated oven, first 10 minutes at 200 C and then another 20 minutes at 180 C
