Proja

Description

Delicious corn-bread-like dish, perfect for a light breakfast or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 12 soup spoons of proja (corn) flour
  • 4 tablespoons of white (wheat) flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup (2dl) of yogurt
  • 1 cup (2dl) of mineral water
  • 250 grams of white cheese (optional)
  • 1 dl of sunflower oil
  • 1 bag of baking powder

Steps

  1. First, and most important, is to make proja for someone you love, then it has a very special taste!
  2. Mix all the ingredients written above in a large bowl, but do not chop the cheese too much - it is better when larger pieces remain. Grease the tray with melted margarine (or molds for bread and muffins, which I often use) and roll out the dough. Bake for 30 minutes in a preheated oven, first 10 minutes at 200 C and then another 20 minutes at 180 C.
